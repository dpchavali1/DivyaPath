Kanchan Varan Viraj Subesa, Kanan Kundal Kunchit Kesa

Shankar Suvan Kesri Nandan, Tej Pratap Maha Jag Vandan

Vidyavaan Guni Ati Chatur, Ram Kaj Karibe Ko Aatur
Prabhu Charitra Sunibe Ko Rasiya, Ram Lakhan Sita Man Basiya

Sukshma Roop Dhari Siyahi Dikhawa, Bikat Roop Dhari Lanka Jarawa
Bheem Roop Dhari Asur Sanhare, Ramchandra Ke Kaaj Sanware

Sahas Badan Tumharo Jas Gaave, Asa Kahi Shripati Kanth Lagaave
Sankaadik Brahmaadi Muneesa, Narad Sarad Sahit Aheesa

Yam Kuber Digpaal Jahan Te, Kabi Kobid Kahi Sake Kahan Te
Tum Upkar Sugrivahi Keenha, Ram Milaye Rajpad Deenha

Tumharo Mantra Vibheeshan Mana, Lankeshwar Bhaye Sab Jag Jana
Yug Sahastra Yojan Par Bhanu, Leelyo Tahi Madhur Phal Janu

Prabhu Mudrika Meli Mukh Mahi, Jaladhi Langhi Gaye Achraj Nahi
Durgam Kaj Jagat Ke Jete, Sugam Anugraha Tumhare Tete

Sab Sukh Lahai Tumhari Sarna, Tum Rakshak Kahu Ko Dar Na

Aapan Tej Samharo Aape, Teeno Lok Hank Te Kanpe
Bhoot Pisach Nikat Nahi Aave, Mahabir Jab Naam Sunave

Sankat Te Hanuman Chudave, Man Kram Bachan Dhyan Jo Lave

Sab Par Ram Tapasvi Raja, Tin Ke Kaaj Sakal Tum Saja
